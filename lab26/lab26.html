Working with different font settings

Control the typography of your website using properties like font-family, font-size, and font-weight to ensure consistency and readability. Use line-height to manage vertical spacing and text-align to control the horizontal alignment of text, improving overall legibility and layout.

Apply text-shadow to add depth and focus to text, enhancing visual appeal.

Use @font-face to incorporate custom fonts and services like Google Fonts to easily access a wide range of typefaces, ensuring that your website maintains its unique design across all devices.
